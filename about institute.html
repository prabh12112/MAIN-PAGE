About D.A.V. College

Jalandhar, Punjab

A century-old institution dedicated to fostering excellence in education while preserving timeless values and nurturing global citizens through holistic learning.

College Overview

DAV College, Jalandhar is a prominent co-educational private institution located in Punjab, India. Established in 1918 by Pt. Mehar Chand in memory of Maharshi Dayanand Saraswati Ji, the college is affiliated with Guru Nanak Dev University, Amritsar and recognized by UGC under sections 2(f) and 12(B) of the UGC Act, 1956.

Mission and Values

The college aims to impart modern education while preserving Vedic values. It encourages innovation and global citizenship through universal and holistic learning systems.

Our commitment extends beyond academic excellence to character building, environmental consciousness, and community service, preparing students to become responsible global citizens.

Academic Programs

  • Offers UG, PG, Diploma, and PG Diploma programs in Arts, Science, Commerce, Computer Applications, Economics, and Business Administration.
  • Comprises 22 academic departments with highly qualified faculty members.
  • Runs a Senior Secondary wing (10+1 & 10+2) under PSEB, Mohali.
  • Admissions are mostly merit-based; some professional courses require entrance criteria.

Infrastructure and Facilities

  • Campus: 88 acres with modern infrastructure and eco-friendly building practices.
  • Laboratories: Hi-tech science labs and computer labs with leased-line internet connectivity.
  • Library: Over 2 lakh books, 93 periodicals, DELNET access for research.
  • Classrooms: Spacious smart classrooms, some ICT-enabled for enhanced learning.
  • Hostels: 5 hostels (2 boys, 2 girls) with modern amenities and recreational facilities.
  • Sports: Comprehensive facilities for swimming, shooting, football, cricket, tennis, and more.
  • Other Facilities: Auditorium, canteens, seminar halls, open-air theatre, Yagyashala.
  • Environmental Initiatives: Eco-friendly building practices and sustainability programs.

Student Life & Holistic Development

The college promotes co-curricular activities through 31 societies, clubs, and associations. Our hostels nurture mutual respect and cultural diversity through various events and competitions.

Community service and environmental education are key values integrated into student life, fostering social responsibility and environmental consciousness among students.

Notable Alumni

  • Science: Dr. Har Gobind Khorana (Nobel Laureate in Physiology or Medicine)
  • Music & Arts: Jagjit Singh (Ghazal King), Hans Raj Hans, Sukhwinder Singh
  • Medicine: Dr. Harvinder Sahota, Dr. K.K. Talwar
  • Public Service: Sharat Sabharwal (Former Ambassador to Pakistan)

Recognitions and Achievements

  • Ranked among top 10 colleges in Northern India by The Tribune (2018).
  • NAAC-accredited with CGPA of 3.76, reflecting our commitment to quality education.
  • First institution to create "Navgraha Vatika" with copyright-registered design.
  • Physics Department holds a patent on solar hydrogen production technology.

DAV College, Jalandhar continues to foster a culture of excellence, innovation, and global education grounded in Indian values.
